Videogames are my favorite thing.

Why I like videogames.

I love playing videogames. I like to spend most of my free time playing them.I play games entirely on my PC and online. My favorite genres are role-playing games(RPGs), first-person shooters(FPS), and strategy games. I like playing them because they give me an entertaining challenge and they allow you to experience stories in a unique way. I often go to a website called Penny Arcade to get news about games and to talk about them on the forum there.

Videogame series I like.

  • Final Fantasy
  • Fallout
  • Deus Ex
